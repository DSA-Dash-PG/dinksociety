DSR Dink Society Rating

Your overall performance score for the season — one number that captures how you're playing across all your games.

It blends:

  • Games won and lost
  • How close those games were (point differential)
  • The strength of who you played

It updates after every match night. Higher is better — and it's what sets your rank on the leaderboard.
