🏷 Naming options for the two new ratings
Men's / Women's + Mixed
Shown in this mockup. Label follows the player's gender, matches MLP language, and reads instantly courtside. No new jargon to teach.
Gender DSR + Mixed DSR
One fixed label for everyone. Simpler data model and table headers, but "Gender DSR" is clunkier on a stat card.
DSR·G + DSR·X
Compact ticker style for tight spots (leaderboard columns, mobile tiles). Could be the abbreviation used everywhere the full label doesn't fit.
