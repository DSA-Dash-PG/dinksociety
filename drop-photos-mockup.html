Option 2 · Inline photos

Images woven into the story

Option 4 · Storyline thumbnails

An optional photo on each storyline card

Every storyline block gets a small image slot. Fill the ones you have a shot for, leave the rest text-only. Keeps the lower half of the article visual without much work.

Recommendation

Where I'd start

Ship the cover photo first, it's one field and the biggest visual payoff, then add the Week in Pictures gallery once you know how many photos you realistically get each week. Inline and storyline thumbs can layer on later since they reuse the same uploader.

Phase 1 · Cover photo

One image field on the composer, one hero block on the article. Reuses the existing player-photo upload endpoint.

Effort: small
Phase 2 · Week in Pictures

Multi-file dropzone plus a gallery grid with a lightbox. The crowd-pleaser for game nights.

Effort: medium
Phase 3 · Inline + thumbnails

Insert-photo button inside body fields and an optional image per storyline. Most editorial control.

Effort: medium
