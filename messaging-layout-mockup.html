Messages — 3 mobile layouts

The problem today: on mobile the desktop two-pane collapses to a stack, so the team list sits on top and the conversation falls below the fold — and the broadcast composer eats the top of the screen. All three options below put messages first and turn broadcast into an action, not a static block. Tap around each one.

Option A · Drill-in inbox

Land on a clean inbox of conversations (newest first, unread badges). Tap a team → the full thread slides in with the composer pinned at the bottom; Back returns to the inbox. Broadcast is the 📣 button → a compose sheet. Familiar texting/Slack pattern.

Option B · Inbox + tuck-away compose

Broadcast becomes a single collapsed bar at the very top — tap it to expand the composer inline, tap again to tuck it away. Everything below is the conversation list, visible immediately. Good if you want compose always one tap away without it ever blocking messages. Tap the bar to expand.

Option C · Thread-first

You land straight in a conversation — no list to get past. A team switcher (scrolling chips, red dot = unread) sits at the top; tap a team to swap the thread instantly. Broadcast is a floating button → compose sheet. Fastest if you mostly reply to one team at a time. Tap chips / the 📣 button.
