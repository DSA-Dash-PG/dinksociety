# Waivers — ready to paste into Admin

Two separate waivers, each its own entry in **Admin → Settings → Liability waivers**
(the cards are already there: "The Dink Society" and "Dink House"). Players sign both,
tracked independently. Governing law: California.

> Not legal advice. Have a California attorney glance at both before they go live —
> especially the Dink House one, since you're collecting a release on a third-party
> venue's behalf and they may want their own exact wording (see note under it).

**How to use:** in each waiver card, click into the text box and paste the body below.
The editor understands this formatting — `**text**` becomes **bold**, `##` lines become
headings — so the section labels arrive already bolded. Tidy up with the toolbar
(Bold / Heading / List) if you like, hit **Preview** to see the player view, then **Save**
and tick "Require this waiver." Saving sets the version and forces a (re-)sign on next login.

## The Dink Society — Participant Waiver, Release of Liability, and Assumption of Risk

Please read carefully. This is a legal document. By signing, you give up substantial rights.

In consideration of being permitted to participate in The Dink Society pickleball league — including all matches, practices, warm-ups, and related events (the "League Activities") — I acknowledge and agree to the following:

**1. Eligibility.** I am 18 years of age or older and am participating voluntarily. I am physically able to participate and take responsibility for my own health and fitness.

**2. Assumption of Risk.** I understand that pickleball and the League Activities involve inherent risks — including slips, falls, collisions, contact with the ball, paddles, or other players, overexertion, heat-related illness, and exposure to communicable illnesses — and that these risks may result in injury, permanent disability, or death. I knowingly and voluntarily assume all such risks, whether known or unknown.

**3. Release and Waiver.** To the fullest extent permitted by law, I release, waive, and discharge The Dink Society, its organizers, officers, directors, captains, volunteers, sponsors, and partners (the "Released Parties") from any and all claims, liabilities, or causes of action arising out of or related to my participation, including those caused by the ordinary negligence of the Released Parties. This release does not apply to gross negligence or willful misconduct.

**4. Indemnification.** I agree to indemnify and hold harmless the Released Parties from any loss, liability, or cost arising out of my participation.

**5. Venue Rules.** I understand the League plays at third-party venues, and I agree to abide by each venue's rules and to sign any separate waiver a venue requires.

**6. Medical.** I authorize emergency medical treatment if needed and accept responsibility for any related costs. The Dink Society does not provide medical or accident insurance for participants.

**7. Photo & Media Release.** I grant The Dink Society and its partners the irrevocable, perpetual right to photograph, film, and record me during League Activities, and to use my name, likeness, image, voice, and any resulting photographs or video for any purpose — including marketing, advertising, promotion, social media, websites, and printed materials — in any medium now known or later developed, worldwide, without compensation, notice, or further approval. All such photographs and recordings remain the sole property of The Dink Society.

**8. Conduct.** I agree to follow the League rules and to play in a safe, respectful, and sportsmanlike manner.

**9. Electronic Signature.** By typing my full legal name and submitting, I am signing this agreement, and my electronic signature has the same legal effect as a handwritten signature.

**10. Governing Law.** This agreement is governed by the laws of the State of California.

I HAVE READ THIS WAIVER, UNDERSTAND I AM GIVING UP SUBSTANTIAL RIGHTS INCLUDING THE RIGHT TO SUE, AND SIGN IT FREELY AND VOLUNTARILY.

## 2. Dink House — venue waiver

**Title:** Dink House — Facility Use & Release

> Note: Dink House is a third-party venue you rent. Many facilities require their
> OWN exact waiver wording and won't accept a substitute. Before enabling this,

## Dink House — Facility Use, Assumption of Risk, and Release of Liability

Please read carefully. This is a legal document. By signing, you give up substantial rights.

In consideration of being permitted to enter and use the Dink House facility and its courts, grounds, and equipment (the "Facility") to participate in The Dink Society pickleball league and related activities, I acknowledge and agree to the following:

**1. Eligibility.** I am 18 years of age or older and am entering and using the Facility voluntarily. I am physically able to participate and take responsibility for my own health and fitness.

**2. Assumption of Risk.** I understand that using the Facility involves inherent risks — including slips, trips, and falls; court surface and flooring conditions; contact with walls, nets, posts, fencing, or other fixtures; contact with the ball, paddles, or other players; equipment failure; and conditions in common areas such as parking, walkways, seating, and restrooms — and that these risks may result in injury, permanent disability, or death. I knowingly and voluntarily assume all such risks, whether known or unknown.

**3. Release and Waiver.** To the fullest extent permitted by law, I release, waive, and discharge Dink House and its owners, operators, managers, employees, agents, and landlords, together with The Dink Society and its organizers and volunteers (collectively, the "Released Parties"), from any and all claims, liabilities, or causes of action arising out of or related to my entry onto or use of the Facility, including those caused by the ordinary negligence of the Released Parties. This release does not apply to gross negligence or willful misconduct.

**4. Indemnification.** I agree to indemnify and hold harmless the Released Parties from any loss, liability, or cost arising out of my entry onto or use of the Facility.

**5. Facility Rules.** I agree to follow all posted rules, signage, and the directions of Facility staff, and to use only the areas and equipment I am authorized to use.

**6. Personal Property.** I understand the Released Parties are not responsible for any loss of or damage to my personal property at the Facility, including items left in common areas, lockers, or parking areas.

**7. Medical.** I authorize emergency medical treatment if needed and accept responsibility for any related costs. The Released Parties do not provide medical or accident insurance for participants or guests.

**8. Electronic Signature.** By typing my full legal name and submitting, I am signing this agreement, and my electronic signature has the same legal effect as a handwritten signature.

**9. Governing Law.** This agreement is governed by the laws of the State of California.

I HAVE READ THIS WAIVER, UNDERSTAND I AM GIVING UP SUBSTANTIAL RIGHTS INCLUDING THE RIGHT TO SUE, AND SIGN IT FREELY AND VOLUNTARILY.
