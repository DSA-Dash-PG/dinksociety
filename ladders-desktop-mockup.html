Rules tab

Rule cards + night at a glance

Rules in a 2-column card grid instead of one long mobile stack, with a pinned "night at a glance" summary and a register CTA on the right.

🎾
Round robin, court ladder
Everyone plays every round. Win your court and you move up a court; lose and you move down. Court 1 is the championship court.
🔀
Partners rotate
New partner every round — your score is yours alone. Round 1 seeding uses your Dink Rating; after that, results decide.
⏱️
Timed rounds
12 minutes per round, rally scoring. When the horn sounds, finish the rally — highest score takes the court.
📊
Points & DR
Season points for playing, winning, and finishing top 4. Your Dink Rating (DR) moves with every game, win or lose.
🎟️
Cancellations
Cancel any time before the night starts — your spot reopens and you get ladder credit for a future night (no refunds).
⏳
Waitlist
Full night? Join the waitlist. When a spot opens you get 30 minutes to claim it; inside 24 hours it's first come, first served.
