How it works
Four tabs, two jobs. 🪜 Ladders and 🏆 Leagues are the two products — tapping one makes it the active tab in its lane color, so you always know which game you're in. Home and Me are shared and stay neutral: Home blends both lanes (what's next + winners), Me is your single profile with both ratings (DR + DSR) and your ladder credit.

This replaces the top-of-screen lane switch on mobile — the bottom bar is the switch. Notifications live on the 🔔 bell in the header (spot-opened, "you're in," availability), not in the bottom bar. Web/desktop can keep the top switch; mobile uses this.
