How it reads
The bottom bar stays exactly as it is today. The League / Ladder toggle sits on top of every page and sets your lane — League turns teal, Ladder turns lime, and the whole page (including your rating: DSR vs DR) follows. A league-only player flips to 🪜 Ladder and instantly sees what's open to sign up for — that's your discovery path.

Edge cases handled: Team is league-only, so its Ladder side just points you onward (ladders are individual). Me keeps one shared identity on top, then shows that lane's rating + results below.

One choice for you: the lane is global here (pick once, every tab remembers). The alternative is per-tab memory (Games on Ladder while Standings stays League). Global is simpler; say the word if you'd rather it be per-tab.
