How the merge works: the Format block (courts, rounds, round time, scoring, court names) is what PickleLadder needs to run the night; the Signups & payment block is what dinksociety needs to take registrations and send reminders. Court names are shared by both — one source of truth. "Courts" drives the count of court-name boxes and the default capacity (courts × 4).
