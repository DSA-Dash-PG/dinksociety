How DSR is scored

DSR (Dink Society Rating) is a 0–100 composite of five weighted parts. Ryan's 73.7 breaks down as:

  • Win % — up to 60 pts (the biggest driver)
  • Avg Diff — point margin, up to 15 pts
  • Clutch — close-game performance, up to 10 pts
  • Volume — games played, up to 10 pts
  • Consistency — week-to-week steadiness, up to 5 pts
