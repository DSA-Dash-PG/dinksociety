Player Badge System

Achievement badges for player profiles. Two earnable awards to start — K'CHN Player of the Week and Ladder Challenge Champion — shown in three visual styles, plus how they sit on a live profile. Nothing here is wired up yet; pick a direction and I'll build it against the real player.awards[] data.

1

Player of the Week — three styles

The weekly K'CHN POTW / "Chef of the Week" award. Gold is the signature color so it reads as the marquee honor. Same award, three levels of visual weight.

A · Crest medallion
Premium, collectible feel. Best for a "trophy case" and as a clip-on over the profile photo.
Player of the Week POTW ×3
B · Inline pill / tag
Lightweight, stacks in a row under the player name. The ×N count rolls up repeat wins.
