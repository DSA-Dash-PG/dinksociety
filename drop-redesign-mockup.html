The Lead

Week One: Smash Society Opens With a Statement, and the K'CHN Gets Loud

Two perfect sweeps, one gritty upset, and a 1-for-11 night that still found its silver lining — Season 1 is officially on.

D
By The Society Desk · Dink Society editorial

If you blinked during Smash Society's opener, you missed the whole thing. They ran Timog Cal off the courts 4–0 and finished the night 11 games to 1 — the kind of margin that makes a scoreboard operator check the batteries.

But Smash isn't alone at the summit. K'CHN answered with a 4–0 sweep of their own over What the Dink?!, riding a perfect night from Katrina Nicasio to sit second only on game differential. Two undefeated teams, zero margin for error — and they meet head-to-head in Week 2.
