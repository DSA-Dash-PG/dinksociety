Why a standalone page works. Ladders is its own simple surface — players reach it from a Ladders link (nav or a card on the league home), and everything ladder lives here behind tabs. v1 ships just Available (sign up + waitlist) and Stats (DR, leaderboard, your nights). Adding tabs later — Standings, Partners, Head-to-head — is just one more button, no re-architecture. Keeps the league app untouched.
