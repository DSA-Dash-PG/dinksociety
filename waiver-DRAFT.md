**THE DINK SOCIETY — ASSUMPTION OF RISK, WAIVER OF LIABILITY, AND RELEASE**

In consideration of being permitted to participate in any way in pickleball
activities, leagues, matches, practices, and related events (the "Activities")
organized by [LEAGUE LEGAL NAME] ("the League," "we," "us"), I, the
undersigned participant, acknowledge, agree, and represent the following:

**1. Voluntary Participation & Eligibility.**
I am participating voluntarily. I am 18 years of age or older. I am in good
physical health and have no medical condition that would prevent my safe
participation. I am responsible for determining my own fitness to play.

**2. Assumption of Risk.**
I understand that pickleball and the Activities involve inherent risks of
injury, including but not limited to slips, falls, collisions with other
players or equipment, contact with the ball or paddles, muscle and joint
injuries, heat-related illness, and, in rare cases, serious injury, permanent
disability, or death. I knowingly and freely assume all such risks, both known
and unknown, and assume full responsibility for my participation.

**3. Release & Waiver of Liability.**
To the fullest extent permitted by law, I hereby release, waive, discharge, and
covenant not to sue the League, its organizers, officers, directors, members,
volunteers, employees, sponsors, and the owners and operators of any facility
where the Activities take place (collectively, the "Released Parties") from any
and all liability, claims, demands, or causes of action arising out of or
related to any loss, damage, or injury, including death, that may be sustained
by me while participating in the Activities, whether caused by the negligence
of the Released Parties or otherwise.

**4. Indemnification.**
I agree to indemnify and hold harmless the Released Parties from any loss,
liability, damage, or cost they may incur arising out of my participation in
the Activities, whether caused by my own conduct or otherwise.

**5. Medical Treatment.**
I authorize the League and its representatives to seek emergency medical
treatment on my behalf if needed, and I accept responsibility for any costs of
such treatment. I understand the League does not provide medical insurance for
participants.

**6. Photo & Media Release.**
I grant the League permission to use my name, likeness, image, and voice in
photographs, video, and other media taken during the Activities for promotional
and informational purposes, without compensation. [Remove this section if you
do not want a media release.]

**7. Rules & Conduct.**
I agree to abide by the rules of the League and the facilities, to play in a
safe and sportsmanlike manner, and to follow the instructions of League
organizers.

**8. Electronic Signature.**
I understand that by typing my full legal name and submitting this form
electronically, I am signing this agreement, and that my electronic signature
has the same legal effect as a handwritten signature. I understand a copy is
available to me by email on request.

**9. Severability & Governing Law.**
If any portion of this agreement is held to be invalid, the remainder shall
continue in full legal force and effect. This agreement is governed by the laws
of the State of [STATE], and any dispute shall be resolved in the courts of
[COUNTY], [STATE].

**I have read this waiver and release, fully understand its terms, and
understand that I am giving up substantial rights, including my right to sue. I
sign it freely and voluntarily.**
