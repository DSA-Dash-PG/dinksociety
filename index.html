How it works

A circuit, a standing, and a story.

The Dink Society runs in Circuits — each one a seven-week social league with real competition, real standings, and a drink afterward.

01 · Format

Round-robin into a gold-medal final.

Six teams per division. Six weeks of round-robin, then championship week. Every match feeds your division standings, stats, and Circuit Points.

12
Games / match

Six per round, two rounds. Every game to 11, win by 1.

4
Points max

2 per round for the winner. Ties split 1–1.

2
Captain signoffs

Both captains confirm the score.

02 · A league night

Thursday nights. Same crew. Real stakes.

Every league night follows the same cadence so captains and players always know what to expect.

  • 6:30
    Arrive & warm up
    Check in with your captain. Courts open 30 minutes before first serve. Exchange lineups 10 minutes prior.
  • 7:00
    Round 1 · Six games
    Women's doubles, men's doubles, then four mixed games. Most games won wins the round — 2 points for the win, 1 for a tie.
  • 8:00
    Round 2 · Six games
    Sides flip. Same lineup structure. Another 2 points on the line.
  • 8:45
    Scoresheet & stay
    Both captains sign off. Scores feed the Circuit leaderboard. Then the after-court round, which is where the Society actually lives.
