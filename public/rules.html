01 · Match format

Two rounds. Twelve games. Four points on the line.

Every league match is played across two rounds of six games each. Each round includes one women's doubles, one men's doubles, and four mixed doubles.

Most games won in a round wins the round — 2 match points for the win, 1 each for a tie. Maximum 4 match points per match.

02 · Gameplay

Games to 11, win by 1.

  • All games played to 11 points, win by 1.
  • Rally scoring — every rally is a point.
  • Home team chooses side first round; away serves first.
  • Sides flip for Round 2.
  • Kitchen Line foot faults: first call = warning; second = point lost.
03 · Lineups & rosters

Lock it in or lose it.

  • Minimum 6 players, maximum 12 players per roster.
  • Check in 30 minutes prior to start.
  • Exchange lineups 10 minutes prior to league play.
  • Rotate partners each game — only play with the same partner once per round.
  • Maximum 3 games per player per match night.
  • Non-rostered player = automatic forfeit for that game.

06 · Conduct

Trash talk: yes. Disrespect: no.

The Society is built on mutual respect. Members agree to call lines honestly, keep disputes civil, treat opponents with courtesy, and represent the league well on and off the court.

Swearing, negative taunting, or poor sportsmanship is not tolerated. Members who repeatedly violate the code may be removed without refund.

07 · Disputes

Self-regulated, captain-enforced.

Games are self-officiated. Sideline players may not make calls or coach during play. If a call cannot be resolved between the two players involved, the point is replayed.

For persistent disputes, captains escalate to league organizers. Organizer decisions are final.
