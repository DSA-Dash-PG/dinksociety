01 · Match format

Two rounds. Twelve games. Four points on the line.

Every league match is played across two rounds of six games each. Each round includes one women's doubles, one men's doubles, and four mixed doubles.

Round 2 repeats the same structure. You cannot swap in a second women's doubles or drop a mixed slot.

Home and away roles

  • Round 1: Home serves first, away chooses side.
  • Round 2: Home chooses side, away serves first.
02 · Gameplay

Games go to 11, win by 1.

Rally scoring. First to 11 wins, ahead by at least one. No cap, no win-by-two.

Switch sides at 6

Players switch ends when the first side reaches 6 points.

Standard pickleball rules apply

All USA Pickleball rules govern play unless specifically modified here.

Pace matters. Most venues give us a time window. Keep transitions brisk, exchange lineups ten minutes before start.
03 · Lineups

Lock early. Reveal together.

Before every match, both captains build a 12-game lineup. Each game gets two players from your roster.

Slot rules

  • Women's doubles (G1): Both players must be women.
  • Men's doubles (G2): Both players must be men.
  • Mixed doubles (G3–G6): One man and one woman.

No back-to-back same pair within a round

The same two players cannot appear together in consecutive games within the same round.

Hidden simultaneous reveal

When you lock your lineup, you see your picks but nothing about the opponent. Lineups reveal simultaneously once both captains lock.

Locking is final

Once both captains lock, lineups cannot be changed. Contact an admin for emergencies.

04 · Scoring

Both captains enter. Both confirm.

Scoring is dual-entry. Both captains type their version of each game's score. The system validates agreement.

Round points

Win the round (more games)
2 pts
Tie (3–3)
1 pt
Lose the round
0 pts

Maximum 4 points per match.

Finalizing

A match is final only when all 12 games are confirmed AND both captains submit. Any edit after submission withdraws both submissions.

06 · Conduct

Play hard. Stay human.

  • Self-officiate. No refs. When in doubt, the ball was in — call it against yourself.
  • Pace and grace. Keep games moving.
  • Captain handles escalations.
  • Roster only. No guest ringers.
Repeated unsportsmanlike conduct can get a team removed with no refund. We've never had to and prefer to keep it that way.
07 · Disputes

Resolve it at the court.

Scoring disagreement

If scores don't match, the system flags it. Talk courtside, fix it, re-enter.

Rule question mid-game

Pause. Check the rule. If unclear, replay the rally.

Need an admin?

Email the league or DM @dinksociety.pb. Try courtside resolution first.
