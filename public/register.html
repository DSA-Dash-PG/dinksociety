Liability waiver

By participating in The Dink Society, I acknowledge that pickleball is a physical activity with inherent risks including, but not limited to, injury from falls, collisions with other players, contact with the ball or paddle, and environmental conditions. I voluntarily assume all such risks.

I release The Dink Society, its organizers, sponsors, facility hosts, and representatives from any and all claims arising from my participation, except in cases of gross negligence. I confirm I am physically fit to participate and carry my own health insurance.

Code of conduct

The Society is built on mutual respect. Members agree to call lines honestly, keep disputes civil, treat opponents and officials with courtesy, and represent the league well on and off the court. Trash talk is welcome; disrespect is not.

Members who repeatedly violate the code may be removed from the Circuit without refund, at the organizers' discretion. Photographs taken during league play may be used on league platforms unless a member opts out in writing.

Refund policy

Dues are non-refundable once Circuit I is underway. Full refunds available up to 7 days before the start date; 50% refunds up to 48 hours before. After that, no refunds will be issued except at organizers' discretion.
