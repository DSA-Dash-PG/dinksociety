DSR Dink Society Rating

Your overall performance score for the season — one number for how you're playing across all your games. It's what sets your spot on the leaderboard.

It rewards:

  • Winning your games
  • Winning by bigger margins
  • Coming through in close games
  • Playing consistently, week to week
  • Getting on the court (games played)

It refreshes after every match night — higher is better.
