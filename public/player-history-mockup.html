What this mock nails down

1 · Team → match history, from Games

Every team card is tappable and opens a bottom sheet with the full head-to-head: opponent, final round score, and the game-by-game breakdown.

Try: tap any card on Games
2 · Same from Standings

Tapping a standings row opens the identical history sheet, so scores are one tap from the table.

Try: Standings tab → tap a team
3 · Same from Team → Schedule

The Schedule sub-tab lists every opponent, past and upcoming, each opening the head-to-head.

Try: Team tab → tap an opponent
4 · Cleaner schedule separation

Live (red), Up next (lime), Upcoming (plain), and Past results (dimmed + desaturated inside a recessed panel) are visually banded so past games don't compete for attention. Hovering a past card brings it back to full color.

5 · One-tap Venmo

The pay button uses a Venmo deep link (venmo://paycharge) that opens the app prefilled with your handle, amount, and note, with a web fallback for anyone without the app.

Try: Games tab → Pay $7 with Venmo
