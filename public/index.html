What's the Society?

A LEAGUE FOR PEOPLE WHO ACTUALLY WANT TO PLAY.

"We started this because open play was getting weird and tournaments are too serious. This is the sweet spot."

You don't need another rec league that peaks at matching t-shirts. The Dink Society is structured competition wrapped in good vibes. Real divisions. Real points. Real hardware for the winners.

Every Circuit runs seven weeks of round-robin play across skill-based divisions. Points carry from Circuit to Circuit, so yeah — showing up matters. Your ranking follows you. Your reputation precedes you.

Whether you're a 3.0 still figuring out your third shot or a 3.5+ who lives for the ernie — there's a bracket with your name on it. Come for the games, stay for the post-match trash talk.

A Night at the Society

TWO ROUNDS. FOUR POINTS ON THE LINE.

:00
Arrive & Exchange Lineups
Captains trade written lineups ten minutes before play. Confirm court assignments, get loose.
:15
Round 1 · Six Games
Home serves first, away chooses side. Women's doubles, men's doubles, then four mixed games. Most games won wins the round — 2 points for the win, 1 for a tie.
1:00
Round 2 · Six Games
Sides flip. Home chooses side, away serves first. Same lineup structure. Another 2 points on the line.
1:45
Sign the Scoresheet & Stay
Both captains sign off — either on paper or electronically. Scores go straight into the Circuit leaderboard. Then it's the after-court round, which is where the Society actually lives.
