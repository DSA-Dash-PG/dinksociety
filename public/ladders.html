Ladder rules
How a Dink Society ladder night works
🏓
Games to 11, win by 2
Every game is first to 11 and must be won by 2, played within the round's allotted time.
⏱️
Beat the clock
If time runs out before someone reaches 11 by 2, the team ahead when the buzzer sounds wins the game.
Winners up, losers down
After each round, winning teams rise a court, losing teams drop one, and partners split — fresh teams every round.
🏆
Who wins the ladder
1. Most wins. 2. If tied, highest point differential. 3. Still tied, higher Dink Rating (DR).
⬡
Dink Rating (DR)
A 0–100 skill score from your results — the final tiebreaker and used to balance teams.
💳
Entry & payment
Pay by card (+10% service fee), Venmo (fee-free), or ladder credit. Your spot is held until paid.
🎟️
Cancellations
Cancel and your spot reopens; you get ladder credit for a future night (no refunds).
⏳
Waitlist
If full, join the waitlist — the next person gets 30 min to claim an opening. Inside 24 hours it's first-come-first-served.
