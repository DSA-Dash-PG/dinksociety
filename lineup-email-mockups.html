Decisions baked in — tell me if any are wrong

  • Players marked OUT get nothing. The benched email goes only to players who were available (in, or no reply) but weren't selected. Someone who already told you they can't make it doesn't need to be told they aren't playing.
  • Opponent lineup is never included. Only your own side, so emailing at lock time doesn't break the blind-lineup anti-cheat before the T-15 reveal.
  • Change email diffs against what was last emailed, not against the previous lock. I'd store a notifiedGames snapshot on the lineup record. So unlock → re-lock with no real edit sends nothing, and a captain who cycles the lock three times doesn't send three rounds of mail.
  • Change email replaces, not appends. It shows the diff and then the player's full current game list, so the newest email is always the authoritative one.
  • Partner swap counts as a change for both players — the one who lost a partner and the one who gained a game.
  • No email on draft saves — only on lock and re-lock.
  • Games are numbered 1–12 continuously, not two sets of 1–6. Rounds stay. Round 1 is Games 1–6, Round 2 is Games 7–12.
  • The receipt flags players with no email on file — they're in the lineup but silently got nothing, which is worth knowing before match night.
