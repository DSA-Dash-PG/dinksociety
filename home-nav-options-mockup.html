My take — do C as the front door, A once you're signed in

Keep dinksociety.app/ladders as a dedicated, shareable landing (your instinct is right — it's great for marketing, SEO, and a clean pitch). But once a player is signed in, drop them onto a single home with the top-level switch (A) that remembers their last lane, so doing both never means hopping between URLs. Then borrow the best bit of B — interleave only the winners recap and "this week" moments across lanes for cross-discovery, while keeping schedules, standings, and signups inside their own lane. Logged-out = separate pages; logged-in = one home, two clearly-tagged lanes.
